üí° Tips for Maximizing Profits

  • Optimize Inventory Levels: Avoid overstocking slow-moving items to reduce storage costs and waste. Use the "Best Seller" metric to guide purchasing.
  • Dynamic Pricing: Regularly review market prices and adjust your selling price to maintain a healthy margin. Use the new edit feature to update prices quickly.
  • Bundle Products: Create packages of complementary items (e.g., Sand + Cement) at a slightly discounted rate to increase average order value.
  • Supplier Negotiation: Bulk buy your best-selling items to negotiate better buying prices, directly increasing your profit margin.
  • Track Loss Leaders: Identify items with low margins and consider if they drive traffic for high-margin purchases. If not, consider replacing them.
